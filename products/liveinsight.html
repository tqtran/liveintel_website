LiveInsight

The intelligence, analytics, and reporting platform that sits above all LiveIntel services. LiveInsight converts raw security activity — phishing events, URL verdicts, campaign results, credential scores, and secret access logs — into the dashboards and trends that managers, directors, and clients actually consume.

Core Capabilities

LiveInsight aggregates activity from every LiveIntel service and surfaces the operational insight that turns raw security data into decisions.

📊

Unified Security Dashboard

A single pane of glass across PhishCheck, SafeLink, PhishSim, PassMeter, and Vault. See live threat counters, click rates, credential risk scores, and secret access events without switching tools.

📈

Trend Analysis

Time-series charts expose rising phishing volumes, worsening click-through rates, credential entropy drift, and vault access anomalies — so you can act on trends rather than individual events.

🗂️

Campaign Reporting

End-to-end PhishSim campaign summaries with per-user click rates, credential submission counts, training completion status, and comparative benchmarks across departments and time periods.

🕑

Threat Timeline

Chronological event feed that correlates activity across all services — a phishing email received, its embedded URL scanned by SafeLink, and the affected user's credential strength checked by PassMeter, all in one view.

📬

Scheduled Reports

Automated PDF and CSV report generation delivered on a daily, weekly, or monthly schedule. Reports can be scoped by service, team, or time window and sent directly to stakeholder inboxes.

🏢

Executive Summary View

High-level KPI cards designed for managers, directors, and clients. Risk scores, improvement trends, and compliance-ready snapshots — no technical knowledge required to understand the picture.

How LiveInsight Works

Security activity flows through a five-stage pipeline and surfaces as actionable insight in seconds.

insight-pipeline
Stage 1Event ingestion from all LiveIntel services via streaming API
Stage 2Normalisation, deduplication & enrichment
Stage 3Trend calculation, risk scoring & anomaly detection
Stage 4Dashboard rendering with real-time WebSocket updates
Stage 5Scheduled report generation & delivery (PDF / CSV / API)
